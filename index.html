Workshop on Uncertain Dynamical Systems (WUDS2026)

August 18-20, 2026 | Osaka, Japan

Dates: August 18 (Tue) - 20 (Thu), 2026

About the Workshop

[cite_start]

The Workshop on Uncertain Dynamical Systems (WUDS) is an international event organized by the Technical Committee on Robust Control of the International Federation of Automatic Control (IFAC)[cite: 71].

[cite_start]

The 2026 WUDS will be organized in the week preceding the IFAC World Congress in Busan, and will be held in close collaboration with the JST ASPIRE project "ASPIRE-CPDS"[cite: 73, 75].
