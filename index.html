About the Workshop

The Workshop on Uncertain Dynamical Systems (WUDS) is an international event focused on robust control, optimization, and uncertain systems.

The goal of the workshop is to gather internationally renowned researchers in a friendly atmosphere to share recent cutting-edge research results.
